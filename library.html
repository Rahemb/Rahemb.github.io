Library at OsloMet Campus

The University Library consists of four libraries, a Learning Support Centre, AV services and research support services.

Opening hours:

Students and employees at OsloMet have access to the libraries at campus Pilestredet and at Kjeller from 06.00–22.00. You have to use your card and code to access the libraries outside staffed hours. External users have access when the libraries are staffed.
