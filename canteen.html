Enjoy Delicious Meals at one of many campus canteens at Oslomet

In the canteen you can help yourself to hot food, soup, salad, cold cuts and bread from our buffet, and pay by weight. In addition, you will find a wide selection of wraps and buttered products, tempting cakes and baked goods and good coffee drinks. Everything served in large, bright and modern premises.
